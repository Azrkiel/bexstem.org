Our four-stage gauntlet is designed to forge founders. Each phase tests a new skill, from strategy and building to launching and surviving in the real market.

Stage 01

The Blueprint

Every great venture begins with a blueprint. In this foundational stage, you will architect your success by developing a comprehensive strategic plan and validating your idea with real-world market research.

Stage 02

The Forge

In the Forge, concepts are given form. You will build a working Minimum Viable Product (MVP), focusing on the core features that solve a real problem for your first customers.

Stage 03

The Launchpad

From The Launchpad, ventures prepare for liftoff. You will establish your professional foundation and become a "BExSTEM Certified Venture," ready for market entry.

Stage 04

The BExSTEM Crucible

In The Crucible, ventures are proven. You will enter the real market to acquire customers, generate revenue, and prove your venture can survive and thrive under pressure.
